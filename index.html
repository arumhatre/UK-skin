Psoriasis

Psoriasis

Psoriasis is chronic skin condition characterized by inflamed red raised area that develops silvery scales. Psoriasis can get worst in winter. Trauma and stress aggravate the condition. Psoriasis has social implications and can lead to withdrawal from society, depression, anxiety .Homeopathic medicines being holistic aims not only of relieving symptoms but also reintegration life with normalcy.

Eczema

Eczema

Eczema Atopic dermatitis usually begins in childhood. Common triggers are House dust mites, Pet fur, pollen, Mounds, Soaps, Detergents, cold dry weather, dampness. Research highlights that stress is a significant contributor to eczema through its effects on immune response and skin barrier function. Homeopathic treatment aims at anxiety and stress reduction and minimizes flare ups.

Vitiligo (leucoderma)

Vitiligo (leucoderma)

VITILIGO is an auto immune condition where the brown pigment melanin of skin is not produced as melanocites gradually lose its function or are destroyed. These skin areas appear as multiple white patches. With the success rate of 80%, we ensure a safe and scientific solution for vitiligo.

Acne

Acne

Acne occurs when hair follicles are clogged with oil, dead skin cells or bacteria. Genetics, fluctuating hormonal levels, oily personal care products can cause acne. Untreated acne leaves lifelong scars.

Warts

Warts

Warts are viral skin infections and all type of warts is contagious. It can take as long as two to six months to develop after exposure to virus. The warts can spread from person to person or different parts of body through direct contact with warts when people pick at their warts bite their nails and do not properly wash their hands.

Lichen Planus

Lichen Planus

The causes of lichen planus are unknown that can affect many areas of skin, nails, scalp, genitals and mouth. It usually appears as purplish, itchy, flat, bumps that develop over a several weeks. At Dr Batras, the approach to Lichen planus is by complete homeopathic medical History taking into account past medical history, family history to ensure rapid, gentle healing with minimal recurrence rate.

Fungal Infection

Fungal Infection

Fungal are common in environment and spread by direct skin contact or from soil, contaminated articles, sharing towel etc. Person with weak immune system, diabetes, cancer, illness requiring antibiotics are prone to fungal infection.
